hypercat

Hypercubes of (clumpy) AGN tori

Author: Robert Nikutta

Team: Robert Nikutta, Enrique Lopez-Rodriguez, Kohei Ichikawa, Chris Packham, Nancy Levenson, (and others tbd)

Nikutta+2016

Synopsis: Handle a hypercube of CLUMPY brightness maps. Easy-to-use classes and functions are provided to interpolate images in many dimensions (spanned by the model parameters), extract monochromatic or multi-color images, as well as rotate images, zoom in and out, apply PSFs, extract interferometric signals, etc.
