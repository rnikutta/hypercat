morphology.angle1(a, b)[source]¶

Compute angle between two vectors a and b.

Uses formula:

\[\cos\theta = \frac{a \cdot b}{|a||b|}\]
morphology.angle2(a, b)[source]¶

Compute angle between two vectors a and b.

Uses formula:

\[\tan \theta = \frac{|a \times b|}{a \cdot b}\]

Slower than angle1(), but possibly more accurate for very small angles.
